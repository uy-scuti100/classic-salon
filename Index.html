Traditional Hot Towel Wet Shave

The traditional hot towel shave is an age old classic. The barber will lather your jaw and face with soothing soap, then they will use either a straight or a safety razor to ensure a full clean shave. Following this a hot towel will be placed over your head for the pores to open up, and to allow for maximum relaxation. It's a tried and tested service that comes highly recommended.
